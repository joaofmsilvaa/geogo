Accommodations and Dining in Lauhanvuori Geopark

Lauhanvuori Geopark offers a variety of accommodations, from cozy cabins nestled in the serene wilderness to well-equipped camping sites for those who love the great outdoors. Whether you prefer a peaceful retreat or an adventurous stay, there's something for everyone.

When it comes to dining, the geopark boasts delightful local cuisine made with fresh, regional ingredients. Enjoy hearty meals at charming restaurants that reflect the rich culinary traditions of the area.

Lauha Catering

Lauhanvuori National Park – Kivijata Feast

Savor a vibrant buffet of fresh, locally sourced produce in the serene setting of Kivijata. Carefully curated to reflect the natural bounty of the region, this spread brings together tradition and nutrition in perfect harmony.

Scenic Dining at Lauhanvuori Lookout Tower

Relish a gourmet experience surrounded by breathtaking views. This elegant setup near the iconic lookout tower combines regional flavors with a rustic-chic ambiance for a memorable meal in nature.

Flavors of Kauhaneva-Pohjankangas National Park

Enjoy traditional Finnish dishes inspired by the natural beauty of the park. This wholesome, hearty meal celebrates local cuisine with a focus on comfort, sustainability, and seasonal freshness.

Beautiful Cottages

Forest Bathing in a Traditional Finnish Palju

Experience authentic Nordic relaxation in a steaming wood-fired palju, nestled in the snowy forest. Surrounded by nature, this outdoor bath offers warmth, tranquility, and a deep connection to Finland’s wilderness traditions.

Authentic Cottage Kitchen with Nordic Charm

Cook and dine in a warm, inviting kitchen that blends traditional Finnish design with modern convenience. This fully equipped space is perfect for preparing hearty meals after a day of outdoor exploration, surrounded by natural wood textures and a cozy atmosphere.

Hygge Moments by the Cottage Fireplace

Reconnect and unwind in the comfort of a rustic Finnish cottage, where the glow of the fireplace creates the perfect setting for slow evenings and shared stories. Embrace the essence of hygge with warmth, simplicity, and authentic northern hospitality.

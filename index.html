Lauhanvuoren Kansallispuiston Kivijata

Discover the unique geological formations of Lauhanvuoren Kansallispuiston Kivijata, a must-see destination for nature enthusiasts.

Climb the Lauhanvuori lookout tower for breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding landscapes.

Immerse yourself in the serene beauty of Kauhaneva-Pohjankangas National Park, a perfect spot for hiking and birdwatching.
